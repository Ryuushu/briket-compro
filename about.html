Our Story

Committed to sustainability, quality, and global excellence.

Pioneering Sustainable Energy Solutions

Founded with a vision to utilize agricultural waste efficiently, Ignis Briquettes has grown into a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium charcoal briquettes in Indonesia.

We specialize in 100% natural coconut shell and hardwood charcoal, meticulously processed to meet rigorous international standards. Our operations are built on the principles of environmental sustainability and community empowerment.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global partner in providing premium, eco-friendly energy solutions while leading the transition towards sustainable biomass utilization.

Our Mission

  • Deliver consistent, superior quality briquettes to our global clients.
  • Maintain zero-deforestation policies by utilizing agricultural waste.
  • Innovate production techniques for maximum efficiency and low environmental impact.
